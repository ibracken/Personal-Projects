Binary Search Trees

A very useful way to sort and easily look through data

"I had to make one of these for class"
- Ian Bracken

The most important rules of the BST are:

  • Everything to the left of a node is smaller and to the right of a node is larger
  • When inserting into a BST, start from the top and work your way down until you find an open spot
  • When deleting, replace the node being deleted with the largest node in the left subtree
